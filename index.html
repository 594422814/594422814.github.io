Biography

I am currently a fifth-year Ph.D. student in University of Science and Technology of China (USTC). I am luckily advised by Gang Hua and Prof. Houqiang Li. I collaborate closely with Dong Chen and Fang Wen during my internship in MSRA. Prior that, I received my B.S. degree from School for Gifted Young in USTC in 2014.

My research interests are in computer vision, machine learning. I'm particularly interested in the areas of face detection, recognition, synthesis, and GANs.
